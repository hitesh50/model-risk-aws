The "experts" above were shown randomized configurations for hundreds of AWS accounts. They forecasted the likelihood of the following scenario occurring for each configuration:

The panelists assumed the account is primary production account, the account is owned by a tech company, and either AWS security or the account owner may escalate the incident.
